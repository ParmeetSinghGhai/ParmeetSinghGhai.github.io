Licenses are generally needed to be saved when you have either installed a new license or downloaded an existing license otherwise once saved, you cannot edit any of the details of a license. Following are the steps needed to be followed in order to save a license.

1

Right after you have added / installed a license or downloaded an existing licnese, you can do any one of the following

  • Click on the "Save" button next to the license email.
  • Click on the button labelled as "Save License" on the controls bar.
  • Right click on the selected license and then click on "Save License(s)".
