Licenses can be deleted anytime should you choose not to use the application anymore. Removing a license not only removes your saved data from persistent storage and local storage but also revokes your google access token and refresh token that the application uses to access your google drive files on your behalf. Doing so will prevent the application from accessing your google drive account in the future unless you decide to add the license for your google drive back again. Following are steps needed to be followed in order to delete a license

1

Select the license that you would like to delete and then you can do any one of the following

  • Click on the "Delete" button right before the license email.
  • Click on the button labelled as "Delete License" on the controls bar.
  • Right click on the selected license and then click on "Delete License(s)".

2

You may also go ahead and take a look at the system messages to make sure that license has been successfully deleted.
