In order to make application functionalities work and to add a google drive account into the application, a license is needed to be added / installed first. Following are the steps needed to be followed in order to add a license into the application.

1

Click on "Licenses" on the application menu

2

Click on "Sign in with Google" on the application menu.

Doing so will make the application first collect your public IP Address using a 3rd party service from http://checkip.dyndns.org. This IP Address is then sent to two other 3rd party services namely http://proxycheck.io and https://ipinfo.io to check and see if your machine is connected to any VPN or Proxy server and to check your IP Address's originating country respectively.

More information can be found in our privacy policy on https://parmeetsinghghai.github.io/psgdrivesync#privacypolicy

3

A pop up window by the title of "End User License Agreement" should show up afterwards. You may go ahead and read the end user license agreement and scroll all the way down using the scrollbar and Check the box saying "I understand and accept the fact that there will be no refind issued under any circumstance(s)".

Tne button "I Accept End User License Agreement" will only be enabled when the above two conditions are met.Once its enabled then please go ahead and click on it ,if you wish to proceed further otherwise you can either close this window or click on "I Decline End User License Agreement".

Please note that all payments should be made to: parmeet.s.ghai@gmail.com.

4

A web browser window should open up next asking you to sign in with your google account to continue with PSGDriveSync application.

Before you proceed further with the sign in process, may go ahead and follow the next step first even though its totally optional. The next step helps with making sure that your google account remains safe and protected.

5

Copy the sign in link from the web browser window and paste it in the box labelled "Other url". The application will then compare this url to the sign in url that is generated with the application. This makes sure that nobody else had sent you that sign url on your computer rather it was just PSGDriveSync application.

Doing so will help put your mind at ease knowing that you are logging into the right url.

6

After you have successfully signed into your google account then the application will display your logged in email right next to the "Sign in with Google" button. This acts as a confirmation on whether you have logged into the right google account or not.

7

You can now click on the button saying "Refresh Available License(s)" and that should populate the area below with all the available PSGDriveSync licenses that you can choose from.

Please select one of those licenses and proceed further.

8

Enter your paypal email address in the box labelled "Enter Paypal Email Address" and hit "Send Code". This will send a unique verification code to your paypal email address which could be from any email service of your choosing like google, hotmail, yahoo etc however please make sure that this is the email address linked to your paypal account thay you used to make a payment towards our paypal account which is: parmeet.s.ghai@gmail.com otherwise your transaction will not show up in the next step.

9

You can now search your payment transaction by first providing the start date and end date which encloses the actual date of your transaction. For example, if your transaction was made on 16th of Feb 2023 then your start date should be 17th of Feb 2023 and end date should be either 18th of Feb 2023 or today' date whichever falls earlier.

Next you can click on "Find Transaction(s)" button. Doing so should show up the transaction details for the payment that you made towards our paypal account which is: parmeet.s.ghai@gmail.com. After that you can select that transaction and click on "Add New License" button

Please do go through our privacy policy at https://parmeetsinghghai.github.io/psgdrivesync#privacypolicy before making any payments as there are few countries which are restricted from purchasing a license from us.

10

After clicking on "Add New License", the license panel should populate with all the license details that you had selected below in the "New License Form". Few important details to consider are

  • License Email - this is the email that you signed into during initial stages of license installation process.

  • Transaction Email - this is your paypal email address that you used to make a payment towards our paypal account which is: parmeet.s.ghai@gmail.com

  • License Start Date - this is date when you made your paypal payment towards the purchase of PSGDriveSync application license. License expiration is calculated as License Start Date + the Number of Days the License is valid for.

11

One last step left now is to simply save the license by clicking on "Save" button next to license email or the button labelled as "Save License".
