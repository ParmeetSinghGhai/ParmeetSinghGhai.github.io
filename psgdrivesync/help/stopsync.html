"Stop Sync" operation allows you to stop sync operations on a drive. Whenever sync operations are stopped on all drives then the following functionalities get enabled back on.

  • "Encryption" window becomes accessible again.
  • "Filters" window becomes accessible again.
  • "Change Master Key" functionality in "Help" window becomes accessible again.
  • Save and Deletion for Sync Containers and Sync Items are enabled back again in "Files" window

1

Go to "Files" window and click on "Switch To Sync Window" button on the control bar of the drive that you need to work with.

2

Click on the "Stop Sync" button on the control bar.

3

All the functionalities mentioned above should show as Enabled or Accessible. The same should reflect in system messages as well.
